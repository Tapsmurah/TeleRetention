Welcome to TeleRetention

The Customer Retention Prediction and Product Recommendation System, a comprehensive machine learning-based solution designed specifically for telecom companies to help reduce customer churn and improve customer satisfaction through personalized product recommendations.

Our Mission

Our system aims to provide telecom providers with an advanced tool that predicts when customers are likely to leave (churn) and recommends tailored products or services to keep them engaged. By leveraging the power of data analytics and machine learning, the system empowers businesses to make informed decisions that can enhance customer retention rates and boost overall business performance.

Key Features

  • Customer Churn Prediction
  • Using machine learning models, the system identifies customers at risk of leaving by analyzing patterns in their behavior, such as usage data, billing information, and service preferences. Our XGBoost model ensures high accuracy in predicting churn, allowing telecom companies to proactively address customer needs.
  • Product Recommendation Engine
  • Once a customer is flagged as at risk, the system uses collaborative filtering KNN algorithms to recommend personalized offers, such as discounted data bundles, voice minutes, or loyalty program benefits, to improve customer retention.
